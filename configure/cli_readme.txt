Yggdrasil Decision Forests
==========================

**Yggdrasil Decision Forests** (**YDF**) is a collection of state-of-the-art
algorithms for the training, serving and interpretation of **Decision Forest**
models.

Github: https://github.com/google/yggdrasil-decision-forests
Example: https://github.com/google/yggdrasil-decision-forests/examples/beginner.sh
